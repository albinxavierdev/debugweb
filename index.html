Frequently Asked Questions

What are the prerequisites for joining?
  • Basic computer knowledge and internet usage
  • Minimum 12th pass for most courses
  • Basic English communication skills
  • Dedication to learn and grow
  • No prior coding experience required for beginner courses
  • For advanced courses, basic programming knowledge is recommended
How long are the courses?
Course durations vary based on the program:
  • Full Stack Development: 24 weeks
  • Data Science & Analytics: 16 weeks
  • Cloud Computing: 12 weeks
  • React.js Development: 12 weeks
  • Java Full Stack: 24 weeks
All courses include flexible timing options to accommodate working professionals and students.
Do you provide placement assistance?
Yes, we provide comprehensive placement support including:
  • Resume building and optimization
  • Mock interview sessions
  • Soft skills training
  • Technical interview preparation
  • Direct connections with hiring partners
  • Placement guarantee for eligible candidates
What is the mode of training?
We offer multiple training modes:
  • Online Live Classes: Interactive sessions with real-time doubt solving
  • Offline Classroom: Traditional learning at our center
  • Hybrid Mode: Combination of online and offline sessions
  • Recorded Sessions: Available for revision and flexible learning
  • 1:1 Mentoring: Personalized guidance sessions
  • Weekend Batches: For working professionals
Are there any certifications provided?
Yes, we provide multiple certifications:
  • Course Completion Certificate
  • Project Completion Certificates
  • Industry-recognized Skill Certifications
  • Technology-specific Certifications
  • Internship Completion Certificate
  • Additional certifications from our industry partners
All certificates are digitally verifiable and industry-recognized.
What kind of projects will I work on?
Our project-based learning includes:
  • Real-world Industry Projects
  • Capstone Projects with actual clients
  • Individual Portfolio Projects
  • Team Collaboration Projects
  • Industry-standard Tools and Technologies
  • Live Project Deployments
Projects are designed to give you hands-on experience and build a strong portfolio.
